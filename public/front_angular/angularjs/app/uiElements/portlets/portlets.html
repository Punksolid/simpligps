Portlets Tools

Portlet One

There are 6 tools that you can add:

REMOVE: a confirmation box will appear to confirm deleting.

SLIDE UP: portlets will slide up / down. Just header will stay.

MAXIMIZE: portlets will get all the page width and height.

POP UP: portlets will "detach" from page, that permit move it where you want.

SETTINGS: a dropdown menu that permit you adding other options.

RELOAD: permit to reload portlets content for statistics, messages...

Portlet Two

No needs to insert all tools in each panel and to repeat your code many times. You just have to include panel-controls class to your portlet and all tools will appear. Magic!

If you want just one or two tools, you can add them "manually" by adding it in your panel header

For example here:

<div class="control-btn">
    <a href="#" class="panel-toggle"><i class="fa fa-angle-down"></i></a>
    <a href="#" class="panel-close"><i class="icon-trash"></i></a>
</div>
